Row lanes

A dedicated row-mode demo using constrained card widths, compact copy, and fixed-height media. This is the authoring pattern that makes horizontal masonry useful instead of surprising.

Row lanes grow horizontally. In practice that means authors should give cards intentional inline-size limits, decide how many rows they want, and let the masonry surface scroll, rather than expecting an unconstrained mixed-media wall to stay compact automatically.
